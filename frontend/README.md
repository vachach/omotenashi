# Omotenashi Academy

Omotenashi Academy is an online Japanese language academy for students who want to learn Japanese with a clear path, steady guidance, and a strong cultural foundation.

The project presents the academy as a modern, focused learning platform for people preparing for JLPT exams, study opportunities in Japan, work interviews, or everyday Japanese communication. The design combines a dark cinematic hero, Japanese-inspired visuals, and a clean course structure to make the academy feel serious, premium, and easy to trust.

## Live Website

https://omotenashi-academy.vercel.app/

## About The Project

Omotenashi Academy helps learners move from beginner Japanese to confident communication through organized online lessons. The landing page highlights the academy's main promise: structured Japanese learning with experienced teachers, flexible online access, and practical preparation for real goals.

The website is built around the main questions a new student usually has:

- What is Omotenashi Academy?
- Which Japanese level can I study?
- Why should I choose this academy?
- How can I contact or register for the first lesson?

## Main Sections

### Hero

The first screen introduces Omotenashi Academy as an online Japanese language academy and gives visitors quick access to course information and academy details.

### About

This section explains the academy's learning approach: goal-based study, online lessons, personal progress tracking, and support from experienced Japanese language teachers.

### Why Choose Us

The site presents the academy's strengths, including structured JLPT preparation, speaking practice, flexible schedules, and a study plan matched to each learner's level.

### Courses

The courses are grouped by learner needs:

- Foundation Japanese for N5-N4
- JLPT Intensive for N3-N2
- Study and Work Preparation
- Individual Mentor lessons

### Articles

The articles section introduces useful learning topics such as JLPT preparation, kanji study habits, and language requirements for studying in Japan.

### Contact

The contact section lets visitors request guidance, share their current level, and take the first step toward joining the academy.

## Project Goal

The goal of this website is to make Omotenashi Academy feel credible, welcoming, and easy to understand. It is designed for students and parents who need quick clarity about the academy's value, available courses, and next steps.

The site is responsive and optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
